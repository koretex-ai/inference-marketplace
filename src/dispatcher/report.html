What we check

Read the way a machine reads you.

Crawl

Our crawler walks your site the way a search or answer engine would: robots.txt and its per-crawler rules, your sitemap, llms.txt, and up to a hundred pages of real HTML.

Extract

Every page is reduced to facts — titles, descriptions, canonicals, heading structure, schema.org markup, alt coverage, word counts — measured, not guessed.

Audit

An AI auditor turns those facts into scores, findings and a prioritised to-do list: what helps you rank, what blocks AI assistants from citing you, and what to fix first.
